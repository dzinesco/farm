Panel style & color theme

Dark panels with colored accents — or something else?

A

Dark + purple accent

Dark gray panels (#1a1a2e), purple accent (#6c63ff), white text. Like the preview I showed earlier. Matches the current app feel but refined.

B

Dark + cyan accent

Near-black panels (#0d0d0d), cyan accent (#00d4ff), subtle borders. More "serious CAD" — like Figma dark mode or Blender.

C

Light + steel blue

Light gray panels (#f5f5f5), steel blue accent (#3b82f6). Clean, architectural, like Adobe or SketchUp's light mode.
